\section{about}

I'm a full---stack systems researcher, which places me in a unique position to see synergies that may be missed by looking across
the entire stack. For instance, inexpensive hardware could enhance a distributed system. We can safely cut through opaque operating system interfaces using programming language and architectural techniques. With my colleagues, I prototype and build these systems --- and most importantly, document research in a way that makes it accessible to all of the many disciplines that make up computer science.

My most recent work looks at blockchains, which I view as the intersection of computer science and finance, and clocks, which still have much untapped potential in computer systems.
